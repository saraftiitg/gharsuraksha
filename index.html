The Importance of Structural Protection

Termites originate deep underground beneath building structures. As a property settles over time, tiny, invisible cracks develop inside the foundations, plinths, and brickwork junctions. Termites exploit these microscopic gaps as a private highway to invade your living spaces, aggressively destroying expensive modular kitchens, luxury wardrobes, and fixed door frames from the inside out. Establishing specialized chemical blocks permanently locks out these hidden liabilities.
