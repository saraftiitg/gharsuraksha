Why Structural Protection is Vital

Termites live deep underground beneath your property. As a building settles over time, tiny, invisible cracks form inside the foundation, plinth, and brickwork. Termites use these gaps like a private highway to climb straight into your living spaces[cite: 1].

Once inside, they aggressively destroy expensive modular kitchens, luxury wardrobes, and door frames from the inside out. By treating the structures early, you create an unbreakable chemical barrier that permanently locks termites out[cite: 1].

Our Treatment Core Stages

Pre-Construction Shield

Treating the foundation trenches, plinth backfill soil, and floor slab matrix before casting to form a complete horizontal boundary underground[cite: 1].

Post-Construction Barrier

High-precision drilling (1 foot apart) along internal wall-floor junctions, followed by deep chemical pressure injection and clean white cement sealing[cite: 1, 3].
